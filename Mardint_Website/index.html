ITU - MARDIN INTERDISCIPLINARY EDUCATION RESEARCH AND DEVELOPMENT CENTER

DEFINITION
ITU MardINT interdisciplinary education, research, and development center is an international and interdisciplinary activity center in Mardin, Turkey.

AIMS
 MardINT aims to; host and execute scientific, cultural, and artistic activities; on national and international platforms; plan and organize interdisciplinary studies to preserve Mardin's natural, cultural, and architectural heritage; create an academic base for scientific, cultural, and artistic activities, develop collaboration with national and international scientific and academic institutions, support the city archive by compilation of work on Mardin done in various times.

 PROGRAM
 workspaces. education / seminar rooms. information / document infrastructure. accommodation / catering. secretariat logistic support.

SPACE
 In 2006 Baysal house is handed to itu for MardINT's use by Baysal family for as long as it is used by itu. architectural survey and restitution projects were finished in June 2007. it is planned that MardINT will open its itu Taşkışla office in June 2007, and will start the restoration of Baysal house in September 2007 with a workshop.

 INVOLVERS
 People of mardin, researchers, artists, investors, non-governmental organizations (NGOs), Istanbul Technical University, equivalent national and international academic insitutions municipalities, governership of Mardin, and other state institutions.

MardINT owes its being to the individuals, institutions organisations listed here.
MardINT is a founder member of INCENTRAS (international center for traditional studies. The establishment process of incentras is ongoing).
